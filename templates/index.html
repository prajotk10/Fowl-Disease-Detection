FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our disease detection process, accuracy, and remedies provided.

How accurate is it?

Our disease detection process is highly accurate, utilizing advanced algorithms to analyze symptoms and provide accurate results. However, it is always recommended to consult a veterinarian for a professional diagnosis.

What remedies are provided?

We provide a range of remedies for various hen diseases, including natural remedies and medication recommendations. The specific remedies suggested will depend on the disease detected.

Can I trust the results?

While our disease detection process is accurate, it is important to note that it is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice. We always recommend consulting a veterinarian for a thorough examination and diagnosis.

How can I use the web?

Using our web is simple. Just enter the symptoms of your hen, and our system will analyze the data to detect potential diseases and provide corresponding remedies.

Is it free to use?

Yes, our disease detection web is completely free to use. We believe in providing accessible and helpful resources to poultry owners and anyone who has hens.
