Welcome to SymptoChecker ‒ Revolutionizing Poultry Care

At SymptoChecker, we provide an easy and efficient way for poultry farmers and enthusiasts to detect hen diseases simply by inputting symptoms. With our technology, you can get quick and reliable disease identification, along with personalized treatment recommendations, all in one platform. No need for complex tools or expert knowledge—just the right help when you need it most.

How Does It Work?

We harness the power of machine learning to safeguard the health of your hens. Our advanced platform quickly detects diseases and provides tailored remedies, ensuring your flock thrives. Together, let’s build a healthier future for poultry farming!

  • ❲1❳ Input Symptoms

    Start by entering the symptoms your hen is showing—such as unusual behavior, reduced egg laying, changes in appetite, or physical signs—into our platform.

  • ❲2❳ Detect Disease

    Using a comprehensive database of hen diseases, our system analyzes the symptoms and identifies potential diseases that may be affecting your flock. We match the symptoms to common poultry illnesses and suggest the most likely ones.

  • ❲3❳ Get Treatment

    Once the disease is identified, we provide a detailed treatment plan that includes medication options, best care practices, and preventive advice to help you manage the health of your hens.
